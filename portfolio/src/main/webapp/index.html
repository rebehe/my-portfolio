Work

Hello! My name is Rebecca and I'm a software engineer at Google in Boston. I've been here for two and a half years and I'm starting to feel confident as a software engineer, but it definitely took me some time. I had imposter syndrome for quite a while (and still have it, to some extent). Here's a blog post I wrote during my junior year internship on imposter syndrome. Warning: there are dorky Harry Potter references.

Anyways, more about what I do - I work on a product called Firebase App Distribution. I mostly do backend, writing code in Java. My team is really collaborative and supportive, and we do a lot of pair programming. We also like to have fun. For Halloween, we dressed up as handmaids from the Handmaid's Tale.

When I'm not coding, I'm leading a Women in Firebase Book Club, mentoring awesome students through this amazing program called SPS, and planning cultural events for the Asian Googler Network.
